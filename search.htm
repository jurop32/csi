GET YOU KNOW BETTER YOUR BENEFITS:

DRAG & PUBLISH

Images are processed in accordance with color management rules.

This means that all photo editing is completed on film scanners and graphic monitors, which are calibrated with professional spectrophotometers. The initially assigned color profile does not undergo any further changes and all conversion losses are minimised. Color adjustments are processed on monitors with minimal chromatic aberration; therefore they are faithful in color and tonal rendering. Every photo is professionally prepared to print in RGB color space and has assigned a color profile suitable for its intended use, optimization and the proper setting of corrections of the maximum black and white points while maintaining the largest tonal range. Upon downloading, the client receives a product which is ready for further use without having to do demanding graphics work.

16-BIT COLOR DEPTH

Your choice to download 16-bit high quality image or 8-bit jpg format.

This version is either not usually offered for it due to heavy demands on storage. This version is very important for use in high quality print by demanding clientele because it allows much more larger range of adjustment as compared to 8-bit version of the same image. There are advantages in adjusting photos with a great color depth, but only when the file is available from the beginning of the process. All of our photos originate from professional color reversal films with extra fine granularity, which are scanned primarily into native 16-bit color depth and after the final edits they are software converted to an 8-bit version. Photos from digital cameras are not included in our collection as, up to this point, full-frame digital SLR cameras with 16-bit input color depth are not available on the market.

ONLY OWN PORTFOLIO

We protect your rights: No sharing with any other stock collection.

So there is no risk that different clients from different vendors could download the same rights-managed picture at the same time due to different delays of response from the servers where the photos are stored. Unlike other stock photo providers who expand their portfolio by buying numerous photos from a large number of online contractors and therefore would know nothing about the image origin, we are the sole owners of the original film masters. We have under control the entire process from the initial file source to the finishing touches and therefore can guarantee the customer the highest product quality.

EDIT FRIENDLY IMAGE

Create own template with larger dimensions than original image.

These "edit friendly" photos have color calibration on one or more edges throughout its length, and have fixed and precisely defined RGB values made available to the client after purchase as a bonus. In the graphics application, it is possible to set these values as the background and extension in the master color calibrated edges (i.e. for use in Canvas on Photoshop). The client can then set the size and proportions, which more than meet its purpose.
